Investigation Overview¶

This exploration and visualization study mainly focuses on finding the bike usage pattern and customer habit or routine, specifically how user type, time, gender, and age impacts usage of the system.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area during february. Visualizations below are created from wrangled and cleaned data to facilitate exploration analysis and help discover usage pattern and rider characteristics jumping to conclusions to their behaviors.
